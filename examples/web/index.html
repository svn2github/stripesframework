Stripes Examples

Stripes currently ships with two examples:

  • A simple quickstart example.
  • A simple ajax version of the quickstart example.
  • A more involved, but still standalone example application called Bugzooky.

For those of you too young or too old or who did not grow up with western cartoons as a kid, Godzooky (alternative spelling: Godzookie) was the diminutive offspring of Godzilla in the 1970/80s Godzilla cartoons by Hanna Barbera. The name from the example application comes from the fact that it is a example bug tracking application, and is massively smaller than Bugzilla.
